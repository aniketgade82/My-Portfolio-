About Me

Engineering Cloud Innovation with Purpose and Precision

I am Aniket Gade, an aspiring AWS Cloud Engineer and final-year B.Tech student in Electronics & Computer Engineering. My expertise lies in cloud infrastructure, DevOps pipelines, and automation using AWS. With practical experience in deploying scalable solutions using services like EC2, S3, Route 53, Lambda, and CloudWatch, I thrive at the intersection of engineering precision and modern cloud innovation.

My hands-on projects reflect a strong foundation in AWS — from hosting responsive static websites to deploying EC2-based applications, building serverless systems with Lambda, and integrating real-time monitoring using CloudWatch and SNS. These solutions not only demonstrate my technical skills but also my ability to drive outcomes in fast-paced environments.

Beyond cloud, I bring leadership experience through national-level competitions such as SAEINDIA TIFAN, where I’ve contributed in multiple roles — from team member to team captain — consistently achieving top ranks. I also actively participate in organizing seminars and managing discipline committees during annual college events, showcasing my collaborative and organizational strengths.

Currently based in Hyderabad, I am on a mission to merge cloud engineering, automation, and agile principles to build innovative solutions that are secure, scalable, and impactful. I believe in lifelong learning and am eager to contribute my skills in real-world cloud transformation journeys.
